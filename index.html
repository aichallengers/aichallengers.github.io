Who can participate?

The AI competition welcomes participants from diverse backgrounds and skill sets who are enthusiastic about artificial intelligence and its applications. Whether you are a seasoned AI practitioner, a student with a passion for machine learning, or an aspiring data scientist, this competition provides a platform to showcase your talent and compete with fellow AI enthusiasts. Participants can include individuals, teams, researchers, and professionals who possess knowledge and expertise in the field of AI. It is an opportunity to put your skills to the test, solve challenging problems, and demonstrate innovative approaches in various AI domains such as computer vision, natural language processing, robotics, or data analysis.

What do you need to build?

To participate in an AI competition, you will need to develop a robust and innovative AI model or solution that addresses the problem or challenge. There is no specific topics, The competition encourages you give it a shoot.

What to submit?

When participating in an AI competition, you will typically be required to submit a YouTube video within 5 mins that demonstrates your project and showcases its functionality, along with the source code for your solution.

What are the judging rules?

We Jugde From 3 Aspects:

1. Problem Understanding: How well did the participants understand the problem statement and its requirements? Did they demonstrate a clear comprehension of the task at hand and the challenges involved?

2. Innovation and Creativity: Did the participants demonstrate innovative and creative approaches in solving the problem? Did they propose novel techniques, algorithms, or methodologies that go beyond traditional solutions?

3. Technical Implementation: How well did the participants implement their solution technically? Was the code well-structured, readable, and maintainable? Did they leverage appropriate algorithms, frameworks, or libraries?
